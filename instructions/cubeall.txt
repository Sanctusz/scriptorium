Included in this script are these Cube World scripts:
Auto Walk
Auto Craft
Fast Reconnect
+50 stats (left clicks 50 times)
-50 stats (right clicks 50 times)

Key to Auto Walk : Numpad Multiply (*)
Key to Auto Craft : Numpad Divide (/)
Key to Fast Reconnect : Alt + C
Key to +50 stats : Numpad Add (+)
Key to -50 stats : Numpad Subtract (-)
If at anytime you want to close the program simply press F8.
