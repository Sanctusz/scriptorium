Script is made for 1920x1080 Resolution in mind (Windowed FullScreen).
Recommend being Scrolled out completely and have all the points u want visible.
This was made for pre-desired path in races.

Shift + x (inside Skill Tree) to save location of skillpoint.
Shift + q to level up skills saved.
F7 ingame then open new Notepad to export saved locations for ready made script.
F8 to crash the program.
