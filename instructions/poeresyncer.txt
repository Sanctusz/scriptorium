Very useful for staying alive in Path of Exile.

Press Ctrl + z to Activate the script and choose an interval between ReSyncs.
Press Numapd 7 to turn the script ON (It will start doing /oos immediately.
Press Numpad 8 to turn the script OFF.
It keeps doing /oos even when in main menu so remember to turn it off while not playing.
Press F7 to Pause the Script.
Press F8 to Exit the Application(Script).
